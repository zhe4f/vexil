## English

> Concurrent multi-connection · Resumable transfer · Streaming checksum · TLS encryption · Cross-platform

Vexil is a high-performance LAN file transfer tool. Through optimizations like multi-connection concurrency and sliding window, it maxes out your network bandwidth.

---

### Why Vexil

- **Fast** — 4-16 concurrent TCP connections, saturating gigabit networks at 110+ MB/s
- **Reliable** — Automatically saves transfer progress, SHA-256 streaming checksum eliminates post-transfer waiting
- **Secure** — Built-in TLS encryption protects your data in transit
- **Simple** — Single executable, download and run — no installation required
- **Polished** — Dark/Light themes, clean design with no ads

---

### Unique Advantages

| Feature | Vexil | LocalSend | Snapdrop | Landrop |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|---------|
| Multi-connection concurrency | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Resumable transfer | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| SHA-256 checksum | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| TLS encryption | ✅ | ✅ | - | ❌ |
| CLI mode | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Tunable performance | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

---

### Quick Start

Double-click to open, then click 「Send」 or 「Receive」。

The first launch will show a quick guide:

- 📤 **Send** — Select files → Choose device → Start transfer
- 📥 **Receive** — Set port and save directory → Wait for connection
- 🔄 Resumable transfer — automatically resumes after interruption
- 🔒 Built-in TLS encryption secures your transfer content
- 🌓 Switch between light/dark themes in the top-right corner
- ⚙ Click the gear icon in the top-right corner to access settings

## 中文

> 多连接并发 · 断点续传 · 流式校验 · TLS 加密 · 跨平台

Vexil 是一个高性能的局域网文件传输工具。通过多连接并发和滑动窗口等优化技术，榨干你的网络带宽。

---

### 为什么选 Vexil

- **快** — 4-16 条 TCP 连接并发传输，千兆网跑满 110+ MB/s
- **稳** — 自动保存传输进度，SHA-256 流式校验拒绝传完等待
- **安全** — 内置 TLS 加密，保护数据传输
- **简** — 单文件可执行程序，下载即用，无需安装
- **美** — 暗色/亮色双主题，设计简约无广告

---

### 快速开始

双击打开，点「发送」或「接收」。

首次启动会显示使用指南：

- 📤 **发送** — 选择文件 → 选择设备 → 开始发送
- 📥 **接收** — 设置端口和目录 → 等待连接
- 🔄 支持断点续传，关闭后自动恢复
- 🔒 内置 TLS 加密，加密传输内容
- 🌓 右上角切换亮色/暗色主题
